Privacy Policy

Discbro collects and uses information you provide us to make the app function. This includes:

  • your username and password
  • the servers and channels you create
  • the messages you send

Without this data, the app can not provide it's base functionality and these are therefore nesecarry.


Additional data we collect regarding statistics around use of our app is optional. This data includes:

  • when you are using the app (to display status)

We also let users input their email. This is optional, but allows for more functionalty, like:

  • Two-factor authentication
  • Security updates regarding their account
  • Reset password functionality
  • An optional newsletter (not yet implemented)
